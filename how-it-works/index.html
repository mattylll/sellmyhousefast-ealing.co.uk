How to Sell Your House Fast in Ealing

Our straightforward four-step process makes selling your Ealing property simple, with most sales completing in just 14 days from offer acceptance.

Step 1

Step 1: Get Your Free Ealing Property Valuation

Getting started is simple. Contact us by filling in the form on this page, calling us on 0800 012 1007, or sending an email. We will ask for some basic information about your property: the address, number of bedrooms, general condition, and your reasons for wanting to sell. This initial conversation takes around ten minutes and puts you under no obligation whatsoever.

Once we have your details, our Ealing valuation team begins a thorough analysis. We examine recent comparable sales in your specific neighbourhood — whether that is Ealing Broadway, Acton, Hanwell, Southall, Northolt, Greenford, or Perivale — drawing on Land Registry data, local market intelligence, and our extensive database of West London transactions. We consider your property's condition, any unique features, its proximity to Elizabeth line stations and local amenities, and current demand in your area.

Within 24 hours, we will present you with a free, no-obligation cash offer. This is not an opening gambit or an estimate — it is a firm offer that we are prepared to proceed with immediately. If you would prefer us to visit the property in person before confirming our offer, we are happy to arrange that at your convenience. Our valuations are thorough, fair, and reflective of what your Ealing home is genuinely worth in today's market.

Step 2

Step 2: Receive Your Cash Offer

Once our valuation is complete, we will present you with a clear, written cash offer for your Ealing property. This offer comes with a full breakdown showing how we arrived at the figure, including the comparable properties we used and any adjustments made for condition, location, and current market trends across the London Borough of Ealing.

There are no hidden fees anywhere in the process. We do not charge commissions, arrangement fees, or administration costs. The price we offer is the price you receive on completion day. We also cover all legal fees associated with the sale, instructing experienced conveyancing solicitors who specialise in fast property transactions across the Greater London area.

You are under absolutely no pressure to accept. We encourage you to take your time, discuss the offer with family, a solicitor, or a financial adviser, and compare it with any other options you may be exploring. Our offers typically remain valid for 14 days. If you have any questions about our methodology or the comparable data we used, our team is always available to walk you through the details. Many Ealing homeowners tell us that the clarity and openness of our offer process gave them the confidence to proceed.

Step 3

Step 3: Choose Your Completion Date

One of the most valuable aspects of selling to a cash buyer in Ealing is that you control the timeline. Unlike a traditional sale where your completion date depends on chains, mortgage lenders, and the schedules of other buyers, our process puts the decision entirely in your hands.

Need to complete quickly? We can exchange contracts and complete in as little as seven days. This is particularly important for homeowners in Ealing facing repossession deadlines, those who have already secured their next home and need to act fast, or sellers dealing with time-sensitive financial situations. Our legal team and funding are always ready to move at pace.

Prefer a longer timeline? That works equally well. Many of our Ealing sellers opt for a completion four to six weeks out, allowing time to arrange removals, coordinate with schools, or finalise arrangements for their onward move. We have also accommodated dates several months ahead when sellers needed to align with specific personal milestones or financial planning timelines.

Whatever date you select, we commit to it absolutely. There is no risk of last-minute postponements because there is no chain, no mortgage to be processed, and no third party whose delay could affect your plans. On your chosen completion day, the agreed funds are transferred directly via your solicitor and into your bank account.

Step 4

Step 4: Get Paid

On the agreed completion date, the sale is finalised and the full purchase price is transferred to you. Funds are sent electronically through your solicitor, and in the vast majority of cases the money reaches your bank account on the same day. There are no deductions, no last-minute adjustments, and no surprise costs — the amount we agreed is exactly what you receive.

Our conveyancing partners handle all legal paperwork, ensuring the transfer of ownership is completed correctly and all Land Registry requirements are satisfied. You do not need to worry about any of the administrative aspects of the transaction — we manage everything on our side to ensure a smooth process.

Once the funds have been transferred, the property is ours and you are free to move forward. Whether you are relocating within the London Borough of Ealing, moving to another part of Greater London, or starting fresh somewhere else entirely, the financial certainty of a completed cash sale gives you the freedom to plan your future with total confidence.

Our sellers across Ealing consistently tell us that the relief of a guaranteed completion — and seeing funds arrive in their account exactly as promised — was worth far more than the difference between our offer and what they might have achieved after months of uncertainty with an estate agent. We have maintained a perfect record of completing on the agreed date and for the agreed price. That is our guarantee to every homeowner who works with us.
